About me

My name is Mario, I'm a Software Developer originally from Graz, but living in Vienna, Austria.

After finishing my MSc in Software Engineering & Economics at TU Graz I worked as a Full-Stack Web Developer for five years doing mostly Java and JavaScript with a focus on Clean Code and Software Craftsmanship.

In September 2016, I decided to quit my job to work as a freelancer and to dedicate some of my time to working in Open Source.

Currently I work at timeular and I absolutely love it there. :)

In the past, I organized the JavaScript User Group and taught at the FH Joanneum in Graz. I frequently visit and speak at meetups as well.
